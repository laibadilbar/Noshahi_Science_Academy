Meet Our Founder

Muhammad Adeel Noshahi

Full Stack Developer

Muhammed Adeel Noshahi, a visionary software engineer, founded Noshahi Science Academy with the aim of providing a world-class science education to young minds. With a passion for technology and education, Adeel Noshahi has dedicated his career to creating an environment where students can thrive and achieve their full potential.

With a strong background in software engineering, Adeel Noshahi brings a unique perspective to the field of education. His expertise in the latest technological advancements and innovative teaching methods ensures that Noshahi Science Academy stays at the forefront of science education. He believes in the power of hands-on learning and critical thinking, which are central to the academy's philosophy.

Under his leadership, Noshahi Science Academy has quickly become a beacon of excellence, known for its outstanding faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and commitment to student success. Adeel Noshahi's dedication to education and his relentless pursuit of excellence continue to inspire students and educators alike.

Our Vission, Mission & Our Story

Our Vision

Noshahi Science Academy aims to be a beacon of excellence in science education, cultivating a community where curiosity, creativity, and academic rigor thrive.

We envision a future where our students lead the way in scientific discoveries and technological advancements, contributing positively to society and the global community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible education that empowers students to reach their full potential. Our mission is to build confidence in students.

Our story

Noshahi Science Academy was founded by Muhammed Adeel Noshahi, a dedicated software engineer with a passion for education and technology.

With a vision to create an environment where students can thrive, Adeel Noshahi established the academy to provide hands-on learning experiences and foster critical thinking. The academy has quickly gained a reputation for excellence, offering state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to student success.
